Componets of a Search Engine

Robot

A robot is a program that automatically traverses the hypertext Structure of the web by retreiving a web page document and following the hyperlinks on the page . In addition, some robots may access and store the title, meta tag descriptions, and some text on the page.

Database

The collection of informations that is organized so that its contents can be accessed, managed, and updated. The web page that displays the results of your search has information form the database that is used by the search engine site. Some search engines use some of the content received from other search engines. Such as Yahoo! which gets its primary search results from Google.

Search Engine Optimization

Keywords

Spend time brainstorming terms and phrases that people may use when searching for your site. Thses terms are what describe your website or business.

Page Title

A descriptive page title which includes your company name or website name whill help your site market itself. The page title is often displayed in the SERP and saved when a visitor bookmakrs your page. Do not usd the exacat same title for each page of your website, include keyworkds in the page title that are appropiate for the page.

Headint Tags

Use structured tags, h1,h2, etc to organize your content. If appropriate include some keywords in the text contained within the dheading tags. Some search engines will give higher list position if keywords are used in the page title or heading. However, do not spam keywords, meaning repeating the word over and over within the content of the page. Search engines continue to become more sophistricated and spamming can prevent your page from being listed if it perceives you are trying to cheat the system.

Description

Write a few sentences about your website or business that makes it special an why people would want to visit. The description should be inviting and interesting so that someone would choose your site.

Description Meta Tag

A self-contained tag that is placed in the head section of a webpage and is displayed on the SERP by some search engines, such as Google.

Linking

Verify that all hyperlinks are working. Each page on your website should be reachable by a text hyperlink and should be descriptive. Do not use phrases such as more info or clich here. Should use keywords as appropriate.

Images and Multimedia

Search engine robots do not see the text within your images. Configure meaningful alt text for images and include relevant keywords.

Valid Code

While it is not required by search engines that your code be validated, well structured and valid code is more easily processed by search engine robots which could help your placement in the search engine.

Content of Value

Your website should contain high-quality, well-organized content that is of value to your visitors.
